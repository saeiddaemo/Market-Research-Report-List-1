<p><p>The low pressure liquid nitrogen storage tank market consists primarily of two types: regular storage tanks and dry shippers. Regular storage tanks are large, stationary units designed for bulk storage of liquid nitrogen, ensuring efficient temperature control and safety for various industrial applications. Conversely, dry shippers are portable tanks specifically engineered to transport small quantities of liquid nitrogen while maintaining its low temperature, making them ideal for sensitive biological samples and medical applications. Both play crucial roles in the preservation and transport of cryogenic materials.</p></p>
