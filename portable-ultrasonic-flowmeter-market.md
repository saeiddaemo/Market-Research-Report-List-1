<p><p>A Portable Ultrasonic Flowmeter is a non-intrusive device used to measure the flow rate of liquids and gases in various applications. It operates by sending ultrasonic waves through the fluid and analyzing the time it takes for the waves to travel, allowing precise measurement of flow velocity. Its portability, ease of use, and ability to provide accurate readings without interrupting the flow make it ideal for temporary installations and maintenance tasks across industries like water management, oil and gas, and HVAC systems.</p><p>The Portable Ultrasonic Flowmeter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for accurate flow measurement and rising adoption in various industrial applications.
