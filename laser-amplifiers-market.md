<p><strong>The Laser Amplifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Lasers Amplifiers</li><li>Continuous-wave Lasers Amplifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser amplifiers market is primarily divided into two types: pulsed laser amplifiers and continuous-wave (CW) laser amplifiers. Pulsed laser amplifiers generate short bursts of high-energy light, making them ideal for applications like material processing and medical treatments. In contrast, continuous-wave laser amplifiers provide a steady output, suitable for telecommunications and industrial laser cutting. Both types serve distinct applications, catering to varying needs in industries such as defense, medical, and manufacturing, driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
