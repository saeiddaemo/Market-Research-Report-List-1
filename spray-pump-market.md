<p><strong>The Spray Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Pumps</li><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray pump market is primarily segmented into diaphragm pumps and centrifugal pumps. Diaphragm pumps utilize a flexible membrane to create pressure, ideal for handling viscous fluids and providing precise flow control. They are commonly used in applications like agriculture and chemical processing. Centrifugal pumps, on the other hand, rely on rotational energy to move fluids, making them suitable for high-flow applications. These pumps are favored for their efficiency and reliability in water transfer and irrigation systems.</p></p>
