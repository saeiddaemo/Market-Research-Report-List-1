<p><ul><li>Ion-Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Partition Chromatography</li><li>Size-Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Supercritical Fluid Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid chromatography is a vital analytical technique in healthcare, facilitating the separation and analysis of biomolecules. Ion-exchange chromatography isolates charged particles based on their interactions with a resin, crucial for protein purification. Partition chromatography separates compounds via solvent distribution, enhancing drug formulation. Size-exclusion chromatography employs molecular size for separation, essential in biomolecular analysis. Supercritical fluid chromatography combines gas and liquid properties for efficient separation, offering reduced solvent usage, ideal for analyzing pharmaceuticals and complex biological samples.</p></p>
