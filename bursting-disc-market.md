<p><p>The bursting disc market comprises three main types: positive arch, anti-arch, and flat type bursting discs. Positive arch bursting discs are designed to rupture under pressure, providing a reliable pressure relief mechanism, ideal for high-pressure applications. Anti-arch bursting discs feature a concave design that withstands more pressure before failing, enhancing safety in critical systems. Flat type bursting discs offer a straightforward solution with uniform rupture characteristics, suitable for various industrial applications. Each type caters to specific operational requirements and safety standards.</p></p>
