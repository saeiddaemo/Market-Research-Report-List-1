<p><strong>The Laser Vision Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular Vision Sensor</li><li>Binocular Vision Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Vision Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Monocular Vision Sensors and Binocular Vision Sensors. Monocular Vision Sensors utilize a single camera to capture images, simplifying the design and reducing costs, making them suitable for basic applications. In contrast, Binocular Vision Sensors employ two cameras to gauge depth perception and spatial relationships, enabling enhanced 3D vision and more accurate measurements. This distinction caters to varying application requirements, from simple detection to complex robotic navigation and inspection tasks.</p></p>
