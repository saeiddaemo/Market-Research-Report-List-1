<p><strong>The Organic Phosphorus Flame Retardant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reaction type</li><li>Additive type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic Phosphorus Flame Retardant market comprises two main types: reaction-type and additive-type. Reaction-type flame retardants are chemically bonded to the polymer matrix during the manufacturing process, offering permanent flame resistance. They are commonly utilized in the production of various plastics and textiles. In contrast, additive-type flame retardants are mixed into materials without altering their chemical structure, allowing for flexibility in formulation. These are typically used in different applications where immediate flame resistance is required, without compromising material properties.</p></p>
