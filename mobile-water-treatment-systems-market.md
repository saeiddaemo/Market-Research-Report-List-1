<p><p>Mobile water treatment systems are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Membrane Mobile Water Treatment** utilizes membrane technology to separate contaminants from water, offering high purification levels appropriate for various applications.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Resin Mobile Water Treatment** employs ion-exchange resins to remove specific ions and impurities, ideal for softening or deionizing water.</p><p>3. **Filtration Mobile Water Treatment** focuses on physical filtration methods, removing suspended solids and pathogens, making it effective for treating surface or wastewater. </p><p>Each type serves distinct water quality requirements.</p></p>
