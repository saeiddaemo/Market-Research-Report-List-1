<p><p>Procure to Pay (P2P) software is available in two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise P2P solutions are installed directly on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but often requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based P2P software operates through the internet, providing easier access, scalability, and reduced IT costs. Businesses can choose between these models based on their needs for flexibility, budget, and IT resources, as well as data security considerations.</p></p>
