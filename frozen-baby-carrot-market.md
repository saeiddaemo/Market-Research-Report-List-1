IQF involves rapidly freezing individual carrots, preserving texture and flavor, making them ideal for retail and food service. Snap freezing quickly lowers the temperature of the entire batch, maintaining quality and nutrients. Other methods may include blanching before freezing or using air blast freezing. Each technique caters to different consumer preferences and storage requirements, ensuring availability year-round.</p></p>
