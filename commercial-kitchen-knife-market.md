<p><ul><li>Stamped Blade Products</li><li>Forged Blade Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial kitchen knife market comprises two main types: stamped blade products and forged blade products. Stamped blade knives are produced by cutting shapes from a sheet of steel, resulting in lighter, more affordable options suitable for high-volume use. In contrast, forged blade knives are crafted through heating and hammering the steel, creating a denser, more durable blade known for its edge retention and balance. These distinctions cater to various culinary needs, influencing professional chefs' preferences and purchasing decisions.</p></p>
