<p><strong>The Lightning Conductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Franklin Lightning Conductor</li><li>Special Type Lightning Conductor</li><li>Early Streamer Emission (ESE) Lightning Conductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lightning conductor market comprises various types designed to protect structures from lightning strikes. Franklin lightning conductors are traditional systems that utilize a pointed rod to attract and safely channel lightning to the ground. Special type lightning conductors include innovative designs with enhanced capabilities like increased resistance to corrosion or improved grounding systems. Early Streamer Emission (ESE) lightning conductors are advanced devices that emit a streamer ahead of the lightning strike, offering broader protection by intercepting strikes before they reach buildings.</p></p>
