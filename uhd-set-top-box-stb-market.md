<p><p>The UHD Set-Top Box (STB) market encompasses various platforms for content delivery. Cable STBs provide services via cable networks, while Satellite STBs utilize satellite signals for broadcasting. DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television) STBs receive over-the-air signals from terrestrial broadcast towers. IP STBs stream content through internet protocols, facilitating on-demand viewing. OTT (Over-The-Top) STBs deliver content directly via the internet, bypassing traditional distribution. Each market type offers unique features, catering to diverse consumer preferences and viewing habits.</p></p>
