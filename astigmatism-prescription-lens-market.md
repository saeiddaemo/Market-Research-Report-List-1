<p><p>The astigmatism prescription lens market is categorized into single focus, bifocal, and other types. Single focus lenses correct astigmatism with a uniform power across the lens, ideal for distance or near vision. Bifocal lenses feature two distinct sections for vision correction, accommodating both distance and near tasks, commonly used by presbyopic individuals with astigmatism. Other types include multifocal and progressive lenses, which offer a gradual transition between different focal lengths, providing broader vision correction for varied activities.</p></p>
