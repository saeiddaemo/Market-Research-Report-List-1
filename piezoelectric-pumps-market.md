<p><p>The piezoelectric pumps market is divided into two main types: standard piezoelectric pumps and large capacity piezoelectric pumps. Standard piezoelectric pumps are typically compact and suitable for low to medium flow applications, offering precise control and high efficiency. In contrast, large capacity piezoelectric pumps are designed for high flow rates, catering to industrial and specific scientific applications where larger volumes of fluid are required. Both types utilize piezoelectric materials to convert electrical energy into mechanical motion for fluid movement.</p></p>
