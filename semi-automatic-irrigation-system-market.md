<p><p>The semi-automatic irrigation system market includes three main types: sprinkler, drip, and surface irrigation. </p><p>Sprinkler systems distribute water through high-pressure spray, mimicking rainfall and suitable for various terrains. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to plant roots via a network of tubes, helping conserve water and reduce evaporation. Surface irrigation relies on gravity to distribute water across the field, using channels or furrows. Each type provides efficient water management, catering to different agricultural needs and optimizing crop yields while conserving resources.</p></p>
