<p><p>Battery cooling systems are essential for maintaining optimal temperature in batteries, ensuring efficiency and longevity. </p><p>Liquid-cooled systems use a coolant circulated through tubes or channels to absorb heat, making them effective for high-performance applications. Air-cooled systems rely on fans or ambient air to dissipate heat, offering simplicity and lower costs but less efficiency in extreme conditions. Hybrid systems combine both techniques, providing flexibility and enhanced cooling performance, making them suitable for a range of applications from electric vehicles to industrial uses.</p></p>
