<p><p>Manufacturing Execution System (MES) software is categorized into two main types: Point MES and Integrated MES. </p><p>Point MES focuses on specific functionalities, targeting isolated manufacturing processes such as production tracking, quality control, or inventory management, often integrating with existing systems. </p><p>In contrast, Integrated MES provides a comprehensive solution that unifies various manufacturing operations into a single system, enhancing data flow and coordination across the entire production line, improving overall efficiency, visibility, and decision-making within the manufacturing environment.</p></p>
