<p><ul><li>Analog PIR Sensors</li><li>Digital PIR Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PIR (Passive Infrared) sensors market is segmented into analog and digital types. Analog PIR sensors detect infrared radiation changes to sense motion, providing a continuous signal based on temperature variations. They are typically simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, digital PIR sensors convert incoming infrared signals into digital data, offering advanced features like programmable settings and enhanced accuracy. The digital variety tends to support smarter integration with IoT devices, making them ideal for modern, automated systems in various applications.</p></p>
