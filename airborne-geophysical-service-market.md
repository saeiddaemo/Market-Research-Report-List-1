<p><p>The Airborne Geophysical Service market encompasses various types of services, each utilizing different technologies to gather geophysical data. Magnetic services measure variations in the Earth's magnetic field to identify mineral deposits and geological structures. Gravity services assess gravitational anomalies to infer subsurface density variations, aiding in resource exploration. Electromagnetic services use electromagnetic fields to detect conductive materials, including minerals and water. Additionally, other market services may include radiometric surveys and LiDAR, providing complementary data for geological and environmental assessments.</p></p>
