<p><p>The arthroscopes market is segmented into three primary types: knee arthroscopes, hip arthroscopes, and shoulder arthroscopes. Knee arthroscopes are designed for minimally invasive procedures in the knee joint, addressing issues like meniscus tears and ligament repairs. Hip arthroscopes enable surgeons to examine and treat hip joint conditions such as labral tears. Shoulder arthroscopes facilitate the diagnosis and treatment of shoulder problems, including rotator cuff tears and impingement syndromes. Each type contributes to advancements in orthopedic surgery, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
