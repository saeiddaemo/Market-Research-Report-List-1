<p><p>The Laser Alignment Module market is primarily divided into two types: Solid-State and Gas State lasers. Solid-State lasers utilize solid gain materials, providing high efficiency and compact design, making them suitable for a variety of alignment applications. In contrast, Gas State lasers employ gases as the lasing medium, offering longer wavelengths and higher power output, ideal for industrial and large-scale applications. Each type caters to different needs based on precision, output power, and application environments in alignment tasks.</p></p>
