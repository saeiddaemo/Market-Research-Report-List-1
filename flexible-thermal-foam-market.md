<p><p>The flexible thermal foam market is categorized into several types based on their composition. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based foams offer excellent oil resistance and durability, making them ideal for automotive and industrial applications. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based foams are known for their weather resistance and flexibility, suitable for outdoor uses. Chloroprene based foams provide good resistance to heat and chemicals, often utilized in electrical insulation. Other formulations may include silicone or polyurethane, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
