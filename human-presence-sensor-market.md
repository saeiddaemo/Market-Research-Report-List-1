<p><p>The Human Presence Sensor market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **PIR (Passive Infrared) Sensors** detect thermal radiation emitted by humans, making them ideal for motion detection in security and automation systems. </p><p>2. **Ultrasonic Sensors** emit sound waves to detect movement, offering precise occupancy detection in various environments. </p><p>3. **Time-of-Flight (ToF) Ranging Sensors** measure the time light takes to reflect from an object, enabling accurate distance measurement for human detection. Together, these technologies enhance energy efficiency and safety in smart spaces.</p></p>
