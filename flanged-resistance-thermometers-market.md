<p><p>Flanged resistance thermometers are temperature sensors that utilize resistance variation in metals to measure temperature. The market features three main types based on wiring configurations: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire. </p><p>2-wire setups are simple and cost-effective but can introduce errors due to lead resistance. 3-wire configurations minimize this error by using an additional wire for compensation. 4-wire designs provide the highest accuracy, eliminating lead resistance entirely, making them ideal for precise industrial applications. Each type serves varying accuracy and budget needs.</p></p>
