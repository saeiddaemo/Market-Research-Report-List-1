<p><p>The Material Handling Robots market consists of several types, including Articulated, SCARA, and Parallel robots. Articulated Material Handling Robots feature a multi-jointed arm design, allowing for complex movements and versatility in various applications. SCARA (Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arm) robots excel in high-speed, precision tasks, often used in assembly and packaging. Parallel Material Handling Robots utilize multiple arms controlled simultaneously, offering high speed and stability, ideal for handling bulk materials and repetitive tasks in manufacturing settings.</p></p>
