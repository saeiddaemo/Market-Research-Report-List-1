<p><p>The dental elevators market is categorized into winged and non-winged elevators. Winged elevators feature lateral extensions that offer enhanced grip and leverage, facilitating the removal of teeth during extraction. They provide better control in challenging extractions. Non-winged elevators, on the other hand, have a simpler design without these extensions, making them ideal for straightforward extractions where less force is required. Both types are essential tools in dentistry, supporting efficient and safe tooth removal procedures.</p></p>
