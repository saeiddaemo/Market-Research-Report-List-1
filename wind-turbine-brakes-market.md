<p><strong>The Wind Turbine Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Yaw Brakes</li><li>Rotor Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wind turbine brakes are crucial for safety and performance, primarily categorized into yaw brakes and rotor brakes. Yaw brakes control the turbine’s orientation, ensuring it faces the wind optimally, while also preventing excess movement during high winds. Rotor brakes, on the other hand, are responsible for stopping or slowing the rotor blades, facilitating maintenance and enhancing safety during adverse conditions. Both types play essential roles in maximizing efficiency and reliability in wind energy generation.</p></p>
