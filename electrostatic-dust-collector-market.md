<p><p>The electrostatic dust collector market is primarily divided into two types: dry and wet electrostatic dust collectors. Dry electrostatic dust collectors use electrical charges to remove particles from gas streams without moisture, making them effective for capturing fine dust in industrial applications. In contrast, wet electrostatic dust collectors use a liquid medium, typically water, to enhance particle capture efficiency, particularly for sticky or heavy particles. Both types play crucial roles in air quality management and pollution control across various industries.</p></p>
