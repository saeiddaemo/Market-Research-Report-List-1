<p><p>Modular portable cleanrooms are classified into two primary types: softwall and hardwall cleanrooms. Softwall cleanrooms offer flexibility with temporary, lightweight structures using flexible vinyl or fabric walls, making them ideal for quick setups and modifications. In contrast, hardwall cleanrooms feature rigid walls made from materials such as metal or composite panels, providing superior durability and contamination control, making them suitable for strict regulatory environments. Both types are designed to maintain specific cleanliness levels for various industries.</p></p>
