<p><p>The Polyethylene Battery Separator market is primarily divided into two types: Ultra High Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE) separators and Conventional Polyethylene separators. UHMWPE separators are characterized by their superior mechanical strength, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, making them ideal for high-performance batteries, including electric vehicles. In contrast, Conventional Polyethylene separators are more widely used in standard batteries due to their cost-effectiveness and adequate performance. Both types play vital roles in enhancing battery safety, efficiency, and longevity.</p></p>
