<p><p>Flanged butterfly valves are widely used in industrial applications for controlling flow. The market is segmented into two primary types: soft seal and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves feature a resilient elastomer lining that ensures a tight seal, making them suitable for lower-pressure applications with potentially corrosive fluids. In contrast, hard seal valves have a metal-to-metal contact design, providing enhanced durability and resistance to high-pressure conditions, making them ideal for demanding environments or extreme temperatures.</p></p>
