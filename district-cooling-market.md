<p><p>District cooling encompasses various market types, including free cooling, absorption cooling, and electric chillers. Free cooling utilizes natural environmental conditions, like low outdoor temperatures, to chill water without mechanical systems, enhancing energy efficiency. Absorption cooling employs heat sources, such as natural gas or waste heat, to drive the cooling process, offering a sustainable alternative. Electric chillers, on the other hand, use electricity for cooling, providing a reliable and controllable solution for urban areas. Together, these technologies optimize energy consumption and reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
