<p><p>The double-sided bonding tape market is categorized into three main types based on plate side adhesion levels. Standard plate side adhesion tapes offer moderate hold, suitable for general applications. High plate side adhesion tapes provide a stronger bond for demanding tasks, ensuring durable and secure adhesion on various surfaces. Low plate side adhesion tapes, on the other hand, are designed for temporary applications, allowing for easy repositioning or removal without leaving residue. Each type serves unique purposes in various industries.</p></p>
