<p><p>The industrial separators market encompasses various technologies for separating mixtures. Centrifugal separators use centrifugal force to separate components based on density. Magnetic separators utilize magnetic fields to extract ferrous materials. Cyclone separators rely on centrifugal force and fluid dynamics to separate solids from gases or liquids. Gas-liquid separators remove liquid droplets from gas streams, while liquid-liquid separators focus on separating immiscible liquids. Other separators include technologies like ceramic filters and membrane separators, addressing specific separation needs across industries.</p></p>
