<p><p>The flat scroll spring market is divided into two main types: non-contact flat coil springs and contact surface coil springs. Non-contact flat coil springs operate without physical contact, reducing friction and wear, making them ideal for precision applications that require smooth operation. In contrast, contact surface coil springs rely on direct contact to transmit force, offering robust performance in heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on operational requirements, such as durability, efficiency, and design constraints.</p></p>
