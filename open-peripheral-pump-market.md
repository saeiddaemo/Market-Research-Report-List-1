<p><strong>The Open Peripheral Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Peripheral Pump</li><li>Centrifugal Peripheral Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The open peripheral pump market is categorized into two main types: magnetic peripheral pumps and centrifugal peripheral pumps. Magnetic peripheral pumps utilize magnetic drive mechanisms to ensure a leak-free operation, making them ideal for handling corrosive fluids. In contrast, centrifugal peripheral pumps employ a rotating impeller to impart kinetic energy to the fluid, resulting in effective fluid movement for various applications. Both types are designed for efficiency and reliability in transferring fluids across diverse industries, including water treatment and chemical processing.</p></p>
