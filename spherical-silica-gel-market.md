<p><p>The Spherical Silica Gel market consists of various types, including C18, C8, and others. C18 refers to a silica gel modified with octadecyl groups, providing hydrophobic properties ideal for reversed-phase chromatography in organic solvents. C8, on the other hand, features octyl groups, offering a shorter carbon chain that results in faster separation times and improved efficiency for certain applications. Other types may include various modifications for specific uses in adsorption, filtration, and chromatography, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
