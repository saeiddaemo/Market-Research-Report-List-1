R-PET is made from recycled materials, promoting sustainability and reducing environmental impact. C-PET, known for its clarity and strength, is ideal for high-performance applications. A-PET, with its clarity and flexibility, is preferred for various food packaging solutions. Each type caters to diverse consumer needs, focusing on eco-friendliness, durability, and aesthetic appeal in packaging.</p></p>
