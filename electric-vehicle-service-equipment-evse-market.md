<p><p>Electric Vehicle Service Equipment (EVSE) encompasses various charging infrastructure levels. Level 1 chargers use a standard 120-volt outlet, ideal for home charging, providing slow charge rates. Level 2 chargers operate at 240 volts, suitable for residential and commercial settings, offering faster charging times. Level 3, or DC fast chargers, deliver high power (up to 350 kW) for rapid charging, typically found at public charging stations, making them ideal for long-distance travel and minimizing downtime for EV users.</p></p>
