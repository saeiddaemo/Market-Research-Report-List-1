<p><p>The vacuum blood collection system market is segmented into several types, including gel tubes, heparin tubes, EDTA tubes, serum tubes, and others. Gel tubes contain a separator gel that facilitates serum separation after centrifugation. Heparin tubes are used for plasma collection, preventing clotting. EDTA tubes are designed for hematologic tests by preserving blood cell integrity. Serum tubes enable serum collection for various assays. Other tube types may include those for specific tests or additives, catering to diverse diagnostic needs.</p></p>
