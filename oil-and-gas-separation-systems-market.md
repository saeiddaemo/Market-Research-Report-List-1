<p><p>Oil and gas separation systems are crucial for efficiently separating hydrocarbons from impurities. Horizontal separators utilize gravity and allow for continuous flow, making them ideal for high-volume applications. Vertical separators are compact and effective for handling smaller volumes, relying on gravity and buoyancy for separation. Spherical separators combine the benefits of both types, offering high efficiency in a compact design and minimizing pressure drop, making them suitable for various operational conditions across the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
