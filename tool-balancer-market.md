Spring Tool Balancers use a spring mechanism to support and balance the weight of tools, facilitating easier operation. Pneumatic Tool Balancers utilize compressed air to achieve weight balance, providing smooth, adjustable operation. Electric Tool Balancers employ electric motors for precise weight control, offering dynamic adjustment.
