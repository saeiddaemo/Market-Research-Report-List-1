<p><p>Cloud computing encompasses three main market types: Software as a Service (SaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS). SaaS provides end-users with access to applications via the internet, eliminating the need for installations. PaaS offers a development platform for creating software applications, allowing developers to build, test, and deploy without managing underlying infrastructure. IaaS delivers virtualized computing resources over the internet, enabling businesses to access hardware and storage on demand, promoting flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
