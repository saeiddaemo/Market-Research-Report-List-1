<p><p>Modular wall systems are categorized into three market types: permanent, semi-permanent, and temporary. Permanent systems are designed for long-lasting installations, often used in office spaces or retail environments where durability is key. Semi-permanent systems provide flexibility, allowing for adjustments or relocations while maintaining structural integrity, ideal for evolving business needs. Temporary systems are lightweight and easily dismantled, perfect for short-term projects or events, enabling rapid setup and teardown without extensive commitment or investment.</p></p>
