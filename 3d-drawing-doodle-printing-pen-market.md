<p><p>The 3D Drawing Doodle Printing Pen market features various technologies, notably Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and Stereolithography (SLA). FDM 3D printing pens extrude heated plastic filament, allowing users to create solid structures layer by layer, making them versatile for artistic and educational purposes. In contrast, SLA printing pens utilize a liquid resin cured by ultraviolet light, producing high-resolution designs with intricate details. Both types cater to different user needs, from casual crafting to professional prototyping.</p></p>
