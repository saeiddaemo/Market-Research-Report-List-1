<p><p>The fall protection equipment market includes several key types essential for workplace safety. Harnesses provide comprehensive body support, distributing weight to prevent injuries in case of a fall. Lanyards connect the harness to an anchor point, allowing for mobility while maintaining safety. Self-retracting lifelines automatically adjust to the user's movements, offering quick response during falls. Safety belts are simpler devices that secure workers while allowing movement. Other equipment types include anchors, warning lines, and guardrails, all aimed at enhancing worker protection from falls.</p></p>
