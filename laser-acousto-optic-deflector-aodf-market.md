<p><strong>The Laser Acousto-Optic Deflector (AODF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free-Space Acousto-Optic Deflectors (AODF)</li><li>Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Deflectors (AODF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Acousto-Optic Deflector (AODF) market is categorized into two main types: Free-Space Acousto-Optic Deflectors and Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Deflectors. Free-Space AODFs utilize acoustic waves to deflect laser beams in air, primarily for applications in laboratories and optical systems. In contrast, Fiber-Coupled AODFs are integrated with optical fibers, allowing for compact and flexible designs suitable for telecommunications and medical devices. Both types enhance precision beam steering and modulation capabilities across various industries.</p></p>
