<p><p>Power supplies in the telecom market can be categorized into three main types: linear, switched, and battery-based. Linear power supplies offer stable, low-noise output but are less efficient and bulkier. Switched power supplies are more efficient and compact, utilizing high-frequency switching for energy conversion, making them ideal for telecom applications. Battery-based power supplies provide backup power, ensuring continuous operation during outages. Each type serves specific needs, balancing efficiency, size, and reliability for critical telecom infrastructure.</p></p>
