<p><p>High power filters are essential for managing unwanted frequency components in electrical systems. The market includes passive filters, which use resistors, capacitors, and inductors to attenuate specific frequencies without requiring an external power source. Active filters utilize amplifying components to dynamically adjust to changing signals, providing more flexibility and efficiency. Additionally, the market features other filter types such as hybrid and digital filters, which combine characteristics of both passive and active filters, catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
