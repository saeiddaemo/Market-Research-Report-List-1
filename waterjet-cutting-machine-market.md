<p><strong>The Waterjet Cutting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Waterjet Machines</li><li>Abrasive Waterjet Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet cutting machine market is primarily divided into two types: pure waterjet machines and abrasive waterjet machines. Pure waterjet machines utilize high-pressure water alone to cut softer materials such as foam, rubber, and certain plastics, offering precision without heat-affected zones. In contrast, abrasive waterjet machines incorporate abrasive materials mixed with water to cut through tougher materials, including metals and ceramics, making them suitable for a wider range of applications. Both types are valued for their versatility and precision in industrial cutting processes.</p></p>
