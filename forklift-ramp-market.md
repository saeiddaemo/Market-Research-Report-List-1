<p><p>The forklift ramp market is divided into two main types: manual and automatic forklift ramps. Manual forklift ramps are operated by users who physically position the ramp for loading and unloading, offering a cost-effective solution for smaller operations. In contrast, automatic forklift ramps utilize hydraulic or electric systems to facilitate easier access, enhancing efficiency and safety in busy warehouses and distribution centers. Both types cater to different operational needs, balancing budget constraints with operational demands.</p></p>
