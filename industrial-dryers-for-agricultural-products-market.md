<p><p>Industrial dryers for agricultural products encompass various types, primarily hot air drying equipment and heated surface drying equipment. Hot air drying involves circulating heated air to extract moisture from products like grains and fruits, ensuring quality preservation. Heated surface drying utilizes heated surfaces to transfer heat directly to the material, efficiently removing moisture while minimizing energy consumption. Both methods are essential for prolonging shelf life, reducing spoilage, and enhancing product safety in the agricultural sector.</p></p>
