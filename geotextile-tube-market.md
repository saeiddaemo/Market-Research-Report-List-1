<p><p>The geotextile tube market is categorized into woven and nonwoven types, each serving specific applications in construction and environmental management. Woven geotextiles are made from interlaced fibers, providing strength and durability, ideal for applications requiring high tensile strength, such as soil stabilization and erosion control. Nonwoven geotextiles, on the other hand, are created through random fiber arrangement, offering excellent drainage and filtration properties, making them suitable for applications like sediment control and water management. Both types contribute to sustainable infrastructure projects.</p></p>
