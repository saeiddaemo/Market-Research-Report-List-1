<p><p>Cleanroom air conditioning systems are categorized into decentralized and centralized markets based on their configuration and control.</p><p>Decentralized systems feature individual air conditioning units in each cleanroom, allowing for tailored temperature and humidity control. This setup enhances flexibility and is ideal for varying cleanroom requirements.</p><p>Centralized systems utilize a single, central unit to control climate across multiple cleanrooms. It enables efficient management of resources, maintains consistent conditions, and is often more cost-effective for larger facilities with similar cleanroom standards.</p></p>
