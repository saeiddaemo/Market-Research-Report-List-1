<p><p>A Laboratory Information System (LIS) is a software solution that manages and streamlines laboratory operations, including sample tracking, data management, and reporting. The LIS market can be classified into two types: on-premises LIS and cloud-based LIS. On-premises LIS is installed directly on local servers, offering greater control and security, but requiring significant upfront costs and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based LIS solutions are hosted on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial investment, while ensuring easier remote access and collaboration.</p></p>
