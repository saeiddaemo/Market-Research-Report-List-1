<p><p>The neurovascular devices market encompasses several specialized categories aimed at treating various neurological conditions. Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are used to occlude aneurysms, preventing rupture. Cerebral balloon angioplasty and stenting systems enhance blood flow to the brain by opening narrowed vessels. Support devices assist in navigating and stabilizing catheters during procedures. Neurothrombectomy devices are designed to remove blood clots from cerebral vessels, restoring normal blood circulation and reducing the risk of stroke.</p></p>
