<p><p>The VR interaction device market comprises three key types: motion capture, haptic feedback, and eye tracking. Motion capture technology tracks user movements, allowing for immersive interactions in virtual environments. Haptic feedback provides tactile sensations, enhancing realism by simulating touch or resistance. Eye tracking monitors where users are looking, enabling intuitive control and improving user experience by adapting the virtual environment based on gaze. Together, these technologies enrich virtual reality, making interactions more engaging and lifelike.</p></p>
