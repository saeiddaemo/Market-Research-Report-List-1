<p><p>The automotive plasticizers market consists of various types that enhance flexibility and durability in automotive components. DEHP (di(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate) is widely used for its excellent performance, while DINP (diisononyl phthalate) offers lower volatility and improved safety. DnBP (dibutyl phthalate) is utilized for its effective plasticizing properties. BBzP (benzyl butyl phthalate) is valued for its resistance to water and heat. DIDP (diisodecyl phthalate) provides a longer lifespan and is preferred in applications requiring greater stability and flexibility.</p></p>
