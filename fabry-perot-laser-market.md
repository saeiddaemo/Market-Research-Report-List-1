<p><p>The Fabry-Perot laser market can be categorized into two main types: single-mode and broadband. Single-mode lasers emit a narrow spectral output, ideal for applications requiring high precision, such as fiber-optic communications and high-resolution sensing. In contrast, broadband lasers produce a wider range of wavelengths, making them suitable for diverse applications like spectroscopy and optical coherence tomography. Both types leverage the unique resonant cavity design of Fabry-Perot lasers, enabling enhanced performance in their respective fields of use.</p></p>
