<p><p>Dental alginate materials are classified into two main types based on their setting times. Type I, or Fast Setting alginate, typically sets within 1 to 2 minutes, making it ideal for quick impressions where time is critical. Conversely, Type II, or Normal Setting alginate, has a longer setting time of 2 to 4.5 minutes, allowing for more working time for dental professionals to ensure precision and accuracy in creating impressions. Both types are widely used in various dental applications.</p></p>
