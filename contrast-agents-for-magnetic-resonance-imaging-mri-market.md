<p><strong>The Contrast Agents for Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barium-based Contrast Media</li><li>Iodinated Contrast Media</li><li>Gadolinium-based Contrast Media</li><li>Microbubble Contrast Media</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contrast agents used in MRI enhance image clarity by improving tissue differentiation. Barium-based contrast media are primarily used in gastrointestinal imaging, providing contrast in X-rays and CT scans. Iodinated contrast media, mainly utilized in CT, can also assist MRI scans in specific cases. Gadolinium-based contrast media, the most common for MRI, enhance soft tissue imaging and are essential for detecting tumors and lesions. Microbubble contrast media, used in ultrasound, is emerging in MRI for vascular imaging and perfusion studies, offering real-time insights.</p></p>
