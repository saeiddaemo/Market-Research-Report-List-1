<p><p>Helical filters are specialized components used in communication systems to selectively allow certain frequency ranges while blocking others. In the VHF (Very High Frequency) market, these filters operate between 30 MHz to 300 MHz, catering to applications like FM radio and television. The UHF (Ultra High Frequency) range, spanning 300 MHz to 3 GHz, supports mobile communications and satellite systems. SHF (Super High Frequency) filters, operating between 3 GHz to 30 GHz, are crucial for radar and microwave communications. Each segment addresses specific frequency requirements in diverse industries.</p></p>
