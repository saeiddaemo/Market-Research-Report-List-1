<p><strong>The Universal Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical UTM</li><li>Hydraulic UTM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal Tester market comprises primarily two types: Electromechanical and Hydraulic Universal Testing Machines (UTMs). Electromechanical UTMs utilize an electric motor to drive the testing mechanism, offering high precision and flexibility for various material tests. In contrast, Hydraulic UTMs leverage hydraulic power to apply large forces, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications and materials requiring significant deformation before failure. Both types serve critical roles in quality control, research, and materials testing across diverse industries.</p></p>
